How It Works

Three phases. Full transparency.

A step-by-step pipeline that keeps you in charge at every stage. No black boxes, no surprises.

1

Describe

You lead

Tell LaunchHub your idea in plain English. No technical knowledge needed. It asks smart follow-up questions about your audience, features, and goals. You make every decision.

2

Plan

AI + You

AI generates a complete build plan: tech stack, folder structure, parallel tasks. You see everything before a single line is written. Drag, remove, or add steps.

3

Build

AI executes, you supervise

AI forks into parallel paths and builds simultaneously. Watch every file change in real time. See every token spent. Pause, approve, or rollback at any point.

Parallel Development

Context Intelligence
